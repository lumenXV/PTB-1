# Vision

PTB-1 is an AI trading research platform for discovering, validating, and comparing trading strategies with disciplined engineering.

PTB-1 is not a live trading bot. Live trading is a future milestone that can only come after research, backtesting, paper trading, and risk controls are mature.

## Mission

Help a researcher answer one question clearly:

Does this strategy deserve more attention?

## Principles

- Simplicity beats cleverness.
- Research before automation.
- Backtest before paper trading.
- Paper trade before live trading.
- Every trade must be explainable.
- Never fabricate test results.
- Preserve clarity over speed.

## Current Scope

Milestone 1 is a basic backtester. It loads historical CSV data, runs one strategy, and reports simple performance metrics.

No AI, machine learning, Robinhood integration, paper trading, live trading, or automation belongs in Milestone 1.
